How the screener works

HalalGames reads each game against a keyword-driven content engine and community-curated notes, then explains the reasoning in plain language. The goal is not to hand you a verdict to follow blindly, but to surface what actually matters so you can decide.

Faith Impact

Shirk, magic, polytheism, mythology, and idol worship woven into story or mechanics.

Financial Ethics

Gambling, lootboxes, gacha, and card packs that mimic games of chance.

Modesty

Nudity, sexual content, and suggestive character design or themes.

Violence Level

Graphic gore and glorified violence versus stylized or cartoonish combat.

Scores run 0 to 100. Roughly, 75 and up reads as Halal Friendly, 45 to 74 as Caution, and below 45 as best Avoided. Scholars differ on specifics, so treat this as a starting point, not a ruling.
