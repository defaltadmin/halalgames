About HalalGames Guide

What We Check

Every game is screened across four Islamic content categories: Faith Impact (shirk, magic, polytheism), Financial Ethics (gambling, lootboxes, gacha), Modesty (nudity, sexual content), and Violence Level (graphic gore vs cartoonish). Each category gets a safe/caution/haram rating.

How It Works

Search any game title and our engine analyzes its genres, descriptions, and tags against an Islamic content matrix. Results are cross-referenced with community votes and reports. Anyone can vote on whether a verdict is accurate, and report errors for admin review.

Disclaimer

This is a community tool for informational purposes only. It does not constitute a religious ruling (fatwa). Islamic scholars may differ on specific games. Always consult qualified scholars for personal religious guidance.
